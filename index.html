About Dr. Fiaboe

Dr. Komi K. M. Fiaboe is a Togolese national and a distinguished agricultural entomologist and Integrated Pest Management (IPM) specialist. He is a seasoned research-for-development (R4D) professional with extensive experience across Africa and the USA.

Fluent in French, English, and Portuguese, Dr. Fiaboe is currently a senior leader and researcher at the International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) in Cameroon, focusing on transforming agriculture in Central Africa.
